Comparison of Two Crack Analysis Methods in Abaqus

This project simulated and compared two different approaches for evaluating crack behavior in a loaded plate using Abaqus:

  • XFEM: Simulation of crack propagation using the Extended Finite Element Method (independent of the mesh).
  • Contour Integral Method: Post-processing method to calculate Stress Intensity Factors (SIF) and J-integrals for fracture mechanics evaluation.

Both methods were applied in Abaqus. The following videos show the stress distribution and crack development for each method.
